The Commerce Economic Development Corporation is pleased to announce a community job fair scheduled for February 8, 2022, at Commerce City Hall. The event will bring together several of Commerce's largest employers, offering residents a convenient opportunity to explore career openings and connect directly with hiring managers.

Participating employers include Gramazini Stone, Huaru Pipe, Hydro Aluminum, and NEXII, all of which maintain significant operations in Commerce and are actively seeking qualified candidates to fill a variety of positions. These companies represent a diverse cross-section of the city's industrial base, spanning stone fabrication, pipe manufacturing, aluminum processing, and advanced building panel production.

The job fair reflects the strong and growing demand for workers in Commerce's manufacturing and industrial sectors. As new companies have established operations in the city and existing employers have expanded, the need for a skilled local workforce has continued to increase. Events like this help bridge the gap between employers and job seekers, strengthening the local economy for everyone.

Residents interested in attending are encouraged to bring copies of their resume and be prepared to discuss their qualifications and experience. The job fair is free and open to the public.
