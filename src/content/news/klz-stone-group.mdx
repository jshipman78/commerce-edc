title: "KLZ Stone Group Opens Granite and Stone Facility on Highway 11"
date: "2019-10-31"
image: "/images/community/industry.jpeg"
excerpt: "KLZ Stone Group has established a new granite and stone fabrication facility on Highway 11 in Commerce, importing granite from South America."
---

KLZ Stone Group has opened a new granite and stone fabrication facility along Highway 11 in Commerce, further diversifying the city's growing industrial base. The operation specializes in processing and fabricating granite and natural stone products, with raw materials imported directly from quarries in South America.

The facility represents a unique addition to Commerce's economy, bringing an international dimension to the city's manufacturing sector. By importing high-quality granite from South American sources and processing it locally, KLZ Stone Group is able to serve customers across Texas and the broader region with premium stone products for residential and commercial construction applications.

The establishment of KLZ Stone Group in Commerce adds to a cluster of stone and materials companies that have chosen the city as their base of operations, reinforcing Commerce's emerging reputation as a hub for stone fabrication and related industries. This concentration of similar businesses creates opportunities for workforce specialization and supply chain efficiencies that benefit all companies in the sector.

The Commerce Economic Development Corporation welcomes KLZ Stone Group to the community and looks forward to supporting the company's growth. The Highway 11 corridor continues to attract industrial investment, and the EDC remains focused on ensuring that infrastructure and workforce resources keep pace with demand.
