The Commerce Economic Development Corporation is highlighting a major industrial property available in the heart of Commerce: a 309,454 square foot manufacturing facility located at 400 Maple Street. This expansive property represents one of the largest available industrial spaces in the region and is well suited for a wide range of manufacturing, warehousing, and distribution operations.

The facility's significant footprint makes it an attractive option for companies seeking to establish or expand large-scale operations in Northeast Texas. Its location in Commerce offers strategic advantages, including proximity to major transportation corridors, access to a capable workforce anchored by Texas A&M University-Commerce, and a competitive cost of doing business compared to the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex.

The Commerce EDC actively markets available properties like 400 Maple Street to prospective businesses and site selectors at the local, state, and national levels. Connecting the right company with the right space is a core function of the EDC, and properties of this caliber are key assets in Commerce's economic development strategy.

Interested parties are encouraged to contact the Commerce Economic Development Corporation for additional details, including specifications, pricing, and available incentives. The EDC is prepared to work with prospective tenants or buyers to facilitate a smooth and successful relocation or expansion.
