The Commerce Economic Development Corporation is pleased to provide detailed specifications for the industrial manufacturing facility located at 400 Maple Street in Commerce, Texas. This property represents a premier opportunity for companies seeking a large, well-equipped facility with extensive infrastructure already in place.

The property encompasses approximately 47 acres and features a substantial office component of 21,477 square feet, providing ample space for administrative, engineering, and management functions. The manufacturing and warehouse areas feature ceiling heights of 13 to 14 feet in standard areas, with some sections reaching 27 feet, accommodating a wide range of production equipment and storage configurations.

Logistics and material handling capabilities are a standout feature of this facility. The property includes 10 loading docks and 2 ground-level ramps for flexible freight access, as well as a rail spur providing direct rail service for bulk materials and finished goods. Additionally, the site is equipped with 8 bulk silos, making it particularly well suited for operations that involve the storage and processing of granular or powdered materials.

The combination of acreage, building size, office space, high ceilings, extensive dock and ramp access, rail connectivity, and bulk storage infrastructure makes 400 Maple Street one of the most versatile and capable industrial properties available in Northeast Texas. The Commerce EDC invites interested companies and site selectors to schedule a tour and discuss how this facility can meet their operational requirements.
