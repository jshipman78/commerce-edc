Solar 76, an American solar manufacturing company, has established its operations at the 400 Maple Street facility in Commerce, Texas. The company is dedicated to delivering high-quality, cost-accessible solar inverters and energy storage solutions designed to fuel grid independence and energy adoption.

Led by CEO James Showalter and General Manager Don Jackson, Solar 76 is focused on solving universal energy challenges through innovative manufacturing. The company's mission is to make clean energy accessible and affordable, empowering communities and reducing costs through American-made solar products.

Solar 76 is actively growing its Commerce workforce, hiring for a range of positions including production supervisors, inventory managers, quality inspectors, and environmental health and safety professionals. The company's presence at the 400 Maple Street industrial complex brings advanced manufacturing jobs to Commerce and positions the city as a hub for clean energy production.

The establishment of Solar 76 in Commerce reflects the city's ongoing ability to attract innovative manufacturers who value the strategic location on I-30, available industrial infrastructure, and strong local workforce. Solar 76 has also joined the Commerce Chamber of Commerce, further integrating into the business community.

The Commerce Economic Development Corporation continues to work with companies like Solar 76 to strengthen Commerce's position as a destination for forward-thinking American manufacturers.
