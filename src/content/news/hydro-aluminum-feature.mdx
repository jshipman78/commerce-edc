Hydro Aluminum continues to be a cornerstone of Commerce's industrial landscape, operating a facility that leverages state-of-the-art technology to convert scrap metal into premium aluminum products. The operation represents the kind of advanced manufacturing that the Commerce Economic Development Corporation actively works to attract and support in the region.

The facility's innovative recycling and conversion processes demonstrate how modern manufacturing can be both economically productive and environmentally responsible. By transforming scrap metal into usable aluminum, Hydro's Commerce operation reduces waste while producing materials that serve a wide range of industries, from automotive to construction and consumer goods.

Hydro Aluminum's presence in Commerce provides significant economic benefits to the community, including well-paying jobs and a steady demand for local services and suppliers. The company's investment in advanced technology ensures that the facility remains competitive on a global scale while contributing to the economic strength of Hunt County.

The Commerce EDC is proud to count Hydro Aluminum among the city's major employers and looks forward to the company's continued growth and success in the community.
