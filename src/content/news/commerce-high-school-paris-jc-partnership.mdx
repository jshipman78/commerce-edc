---
title: "Commerce High School Announces Partnership with Paris Junior College"
date: "2025-05-23"
excerpt: "Commerce High School and Paris Junior College have formed a new educational partnership to expand opportunities for local students."
---

Commerce High School and Paris Junior College have announced a new partnership aimed at providing Commerce students with expanded access to higher education and workforce training opportunities. This collaboration represents a significant step forward in connecting local students with pathways to college credit and career-ready skills while still in high school.

Through this partnership, Commerce High School students will have the opportunity to take advantage of Paris Junior College's academic and technical programs, helping them get a head start on their post-secondary education. The initiative aligns with broader efforts across the region to strengthen the pipeline between K-12 education and higher learning institutions.

The Commerce Economic Development Corporation views this partnership as a key investment in the community's future workforce. By equipping students with college-level coursework and technical certifications before graduation, Commerce is positioning itself as a community that prioritizes education and workforce development. These efforts are critical to attracting and retaining businesses that depend on a skilled and educated labor pool.

Community leaders and educators alike have expressed enthusiasm about the potential impact of this collaboration on student outcomes and the long-term economic vitality of the Commerce area.
