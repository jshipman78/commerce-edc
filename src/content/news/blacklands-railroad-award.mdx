title: "Blacklands Railroad Named 2011 Short Line Railroad of the Year"
date: "2011-03-27"
image: "/images/hero/commerce-aerial.jpg"
excerpt: "The Blacklands Railroad, serving Commerce, has been named the 2011 Short Line Railroad of the Year by Railway Age Magazine."
---

The Blacklands Railroad has been honored as the 2011 Short Line Railroad of the Year by Railway Age Magazine, one of the most respected publications in the railroad industry. This prestigious award recognizes the Blacklands Railroad's outstanding performance, service quality, and contributions to the communities it serves, including Commerce, Texas.

The Short Line Railroad of the Year award is given annually to a short line or regional railroad that demonstrates excellence in operations, safety, customer service, and community engagement. The Blacklands Railroad's selection for this honor reflects its critical role in providing reliable freight rail service that supports the industrial and agricultural businesses along its route.

For Commerce, the Blacklands Railroad is an essential piece of the city's transportation infrastructure. Rail access is a significant factor in site selection decisions for manufacturing and distribution companies, and the presence of an award-winning short line railroad enhances Commerce's competitiveness in attracting new industrial investment. Several of the city's major employers depend on rail service for the efficient movement of raw materials and finished products.

The Commerce Economic Development Corporation congratulates the Blacklands Railroad on this well-deserved recognition and values the railroad's ongoing partnership in supporting the city's economic growth. Quality rail service remains a key asset in Commerce's economic development toolkit.
