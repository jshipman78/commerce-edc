NEXII, the innovative Vancouver-based building technology company operating under the NEXUS-2 banner, has selected Commerce, Texas as the site for a major new manufacturing facility. The plant is expected to create approximately 200 jobs for the local community, representing one of the most significant employment announcements in Commerce in recent years.

The facility will be dedicated to producing NEXII's proprietary building panels, with a projected annual output exceeding 8 million square feet. NEXII's panels are designed to offer superior performance in terms of energy efficiency, sustainability, and speed of construction compared to traditional building materials. The company's decision to locate in Commerce positions the city at the forefront of advanced building technology manufacturing.

The arrival of NEXII brings not only direct employment but also the broader economic benefits that accompany a large-scale manufacturing operation, including demand for local suppliers, increased tax revenue, and heightened visibility for Commerce as a destination for innovative companies. The project underscores the city's ability to attract forward-thinking businesses from across North America.

The Commerce Economic Development Corporation worked closely with NEXII throughout the site selection process, highlighting Commerce's strategic location, available industrial infrastructure, and strong workforce. This announcement is a major milestone in the EDC's ongoing mission to diversify and strengthen the local economy.
