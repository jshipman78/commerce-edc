BigBattery, a leading American manufacturer of LiFePO4 batteries and energy storage solutions, continues to expand its operations at its Commerce, Texas headquarters. Founded in 2019 and headquartered at 400 Maple Street, BigBattery has grown to approximately 48 employees and ships all of its products from its Commerce facility.

The company recently launched the ETHOS, a new energy storage system that represents a significant advancement in their product lineup. BigBattery has also announced expansion to an additional facility in Commerce, signaling continued growth and investment in the community.

BigBattery provides quality lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries for a wide range of applications, including off-grid homes, grid-tied residences, golf carts, RVs, and other mobile energy systems. The company's focus on American manufacturing and direct-to-consumer distribution has fueled its rapid growth.

The expansion of BigBattery's Commerce operations demonstrates the growing cluster of clean energy manufacturing companies choosing Commerce as their base of operations. The city's central location, available industrial space, and business-friendly environment continue to attract companies in the rapidly growing energy storage sector.

The Commerce Economic Development Corporation welcomes BigBattery's continued investment in the community and the jobs it brings to Commerce-area residents.
