---
title: "Commerce EDC Receives $90,000 USDA Revolving Loan Fund Grant"
date: "2018-03-27"
excerpt: "The Commerce EDC has been awarded a $90,000 USDA grant to establish a revolving loan fund for small businesses within the Commerce extraterritorial jurisdiction."
---

The Commerce Economic Development Corporation has been awarded a $90,000 grant from the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) to establish a revolving loan fund dedicated to supporting small businesses within Commerce and its extraterritorial jurisdiction (ETJ). This grant provides a valuable new financial tool for fostering entrepreneurship and small business growth in the community.

The revolving loan fund will make capital available to qualifying small businesses that may face barriers to obtaining traditional financing. As loans are repaid, the funds are recycled back into the pool, allowing the program to serve multiple businesses over time and creating a self-sustaining source of economic development capital for Commerce.

Small businesses are a vital component of Commerce's economic ecosystem, providing jobs, services, and community investment that complement the city's larger industrial employers. By lowering the barriers to accessing capital, the revolving loan fund will help aspiring and existing small business owners start, sustain, and grow their enterprises in Commerce.

The Commerce EDC is grateful for the USDA's investment in the community and is committed to administering the fund in a manner that maximizes its impact. Business owners within the Commerce ETJ who are interested in learning more about the revolving loan fund are encouraged to contact the EDC for eligibility requirements and application information.
