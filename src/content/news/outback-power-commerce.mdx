OutBack Power Technologies, a manufacturer of advanced solar energy solutions, operates from its headquarters at 400 Maple Street in Commerce, Texas. The company specializes in inverter/chargers, charge controllers, and integrated systems tailored for both residential and commercial solar applications.

With over 300 megawatts of energy-efficient solar systems and battery storage successfully installed, OutBack Power has assisted thousands of customers in achieving energy freedom. The company employs approximately 45-57 workers at its Commerce facility, contributing to the local economy and reinforcing Commerce's growing reputation as a center for clean energy manufacturing.

OutBack Power's product line serves a broad market, from homeowners seeking energy independence to commercial installations requiring reliable, high-performance solar power systems. The company's Commerce location provides strategic advantages for manufacturing and distribution, with easy access to the I-30 corridor and proximity to the Dallas-Fort Worth metropolitan area.

The presence of OutBack Power alongside fellow clean energy manufacturers Solar 76 and BigBattery at the 400 Maple Street industrial complex has created a unique clean energy manufacturing cluster in Commerce — a development that positions the city at the forefront of the American renewable energy manufacturing sector.

The Commerce Economic Development Corporation continues to support the growth of Commerce's clean energy cluster, working with manufacturers to ensure they have the infrastructure, workforce, and resources they need to succeed.
