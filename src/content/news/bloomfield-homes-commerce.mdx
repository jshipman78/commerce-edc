---
title: "Bloomfield Homes to Build 286 New Homes in Commerce"
date: "2022-09-01"
excerpt: "Bloomfield Homes announces plans to construct 286 new homes on 66 acres in Commerce, with an average price of $330,000."
---

Bloomfield Homes has announced plans to build 286 new homes in Commerce, marking one of the most significant residential development projects in the city's recent history. The development will be situated on approximately 66 acres located south and east of Commerce Elementary School, bringing a substantial influx of new housing to the community.

The homes are expected to carry an average price point of approximately $330,000, offering quality new construction that meets the growing demand for housing in the Commerce area. This price range positions the development to attract families and professionals looking for affordable yet modern homes within commuting distance of the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex.

The addition of nearly 300 new homes will have a meaningful impact on Commerce's population growth, tax base, and local economy. New residents will support local businesses, schools, and community services, contributing to a cycle of growth and investment that benefits the entire city. The development also signals strong builder confidence in Commerce as a desirable place to live and raise a family.

The Commerce Economic Development Corporation has been instrumental in fostering the conditions that make large-scale residential development viable, including infrastructure improvements and a pro-growth business environment. This project by Bloomfield Homes is a testament to those ongoing efforts.
